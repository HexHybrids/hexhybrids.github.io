Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

HexHybrids ("we", "us", or "our") operates websites and mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

Our applications are designed to minimize data collection. Unless explicitly stated within a specific app's in-product notices or its Google Play Store listing, our applications do not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information from users.

Where any of our applications require data (for example, to save user preferences or provide a specific feature), this will be clearly disclosed within the app itself and on its Play Store listing.

Use of Information

Any information collected is used solely to provide, maintain, and improve the specific application or service. We do not sell user data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Some of our applications may use third-party services (such as crash reporting or basic analytics) that process information for operational purposes. Where any such service is used in an app, it will be disclosed on that app's Play Store listing.

Data Retention

Any data collected is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information we collect. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
